Product FAQs

How can I extend my phone's battery life?
To maximize your battery, you can reduce screen brightness, turn off unnecessary features like Bluetooth and Wi-Fi, and limit background app usage.
How much storage do I need?
The amount of storage you need depends on your usage.
Do wireless chargers work with any phone?
No. Only phones that support the Qi wireless charging standard can use wireless chargers. Many newer Android phones and iPhones are compatible, but you should always check your device's specifications.
